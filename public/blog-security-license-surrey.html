Security License Application in Surrey, BC: Quick Guide

If you want to work as a security guard in Surrey, obtaining a valid Security Worker Licence is mandatory. This licence ensures that security professionals are properly trained, background-checked, and legally authorized to work in British Columbia.

Why a Security Licence Is Required

A security licence confirms that individuals meet provincial standards for training, screening, and professionalism—helping protect people, property, and public trust.

Steps to Apply for a Security Licence in Surrey

1. Check Eligibility

You must be at least 19 years old and legally entitled to work in Canada.

2. Complete Approved Training

Finish an accredited security training course covering legal authority, emergency response, and conflict management.

3. Criminal Record Check & Fingerprinting

A background check and fingerprints are required to verify identity and eligibility.

4. Submit Application & Pay Fees

Complete the official application and submit it with all required documents and fees.

5. Await Approval

Once reviewed, your licence will be issued if all requirements are met.
