5 Reasons to Pursue a Security Career in Surrey

Oct 15, 2025
4 min read

Surrey is one of BC's fastest-growing cities, and with growth comes an increasing need for professional security services. Whether you're looking for a stable career or a stepping stone into law enforcement, getting your BC Security License is the first step.

1. Unprecedented Job Stability

Unlike many industries that face seasonal dips, the security sector remains vital year-round. From retail centers like Central City to corporate headquarters and construction sites, the demand is constant.

2. Diverse Working Environments

Security isn't just about standing at a gate. In Surrey, you can find roles in mobile patrol, loss prevention, event security, and high-tech monitoring centers. There's a niche for every personality type.

3. Clear Career Progression

Starting as a security guard often leads to roles like Site Supervisor, Account Manager, or Security Consultant. Many of our clients use security experience to strengthen their applications for the RCMP or local police departments.

4. Flexible Scheduling

Most security companies offer varied shifts—days, afternoons, and overnights. This makes it an ideal choice for students or those looking for part-time work alongside other commitments.

5. Skill Development

You'll gain invaluable skills in conflict resolution, emergency response, and observational techniques. These are highly transferable skills that every employer values.

Getting Your License

To apply for a security license in BC, you need to complete the Basic Security Training (BST) and pass a criminal record check (including fingerprints). We specialize in the fast, digital fingerprinting required for your application.
