About Us

Trusted provider of fingerprinting and background screening services.

Overview of Our Business

Welcome to Point Zero Global Services Ltd.

At Point Zero, we understand the importance of maintaining a safe and secure environment, whether it's for employment purposes, licensing requirements, or personal peace of mind. We take pride in offering comprehensive and efficient fingerprinting and background screening solutions.

Our team of highly trained professionals utilizes state-of-the-art technology and follows strict protocols to ensure accurate results and maintain the utmost confidentiality. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our clients.

Whether you are an employer, an individual, or a government agency, you can rely on us to provide reliable and timely information to support your decision-making process. At Point Zero, integrity and professionalism are at the core of everything we do.

Future Focused

Our vision is to become the leading provider of reliable and efficient fingerprinting and background screening services. We aim to create a safer and more secure society by offering accurate and timely information to our clients.
