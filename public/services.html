Service 08

USA Entry Waiver

Don't let restrictions stop you. We provide professional USA Entry Waiver services in Surrey, BC, for those denied entry due to criminal records or other regulatory grounds.

Common Rejection Grounds
  • Criminal record (Moral turpitude)
  • Possession/Trafficking cases
  • Previous deportations or overstays
  • Terrorism/Money Laundering claims
Why Apply?

Cross-border security is critical. Anyone with a criminal record (even if charges were dismissed or stayed) can be denied entry. A copy of the waiver is essential to travel legally into the USA.

Associated Fees

DHS Fee: $585 USD

Application fee varies by case.

Process Detail

Service 10

Criminal Record Checks

Save time and money with our fast, name and date-of-birth based checks. Ideal for employment and licensing requiring basic or Level-2 screening.

Basic Name & DOB Check (CPIC)

A standard check running your name/DOB against the National Police Information database.
*Note: If a possible match occurs, a fingerprint-based check by RCMP will be required.

Judicial Matters Check

An enhanced Level-2 screening suitable for many employment and licensing needs.

Fast Processing (< 1 Hour) Employment Ready

Some local and foreign authorities do not accept basic checks. If a name match is found, or for specific requirements, a full fingerprint-based check is mandatory.
